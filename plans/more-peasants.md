## Implementation Notes
- Split into two region-specific specialists to prevent game-breaking cross-region conflicts
- Each specialist targets only their region's Tier 3 & 4 buildings
- Each specialist adds only their region's peasant workforce
- Players need both if managing both Latium and Albion higher-tier buildings
- Historical names: Sextus (sixth) and Decimus (tenth) - common Roman praenomina
